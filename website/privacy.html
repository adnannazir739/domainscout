Privacy Policy

Effective: June 23, 2026

This policy reflects the current pre-release product. It must be reviewed again before enabling analytics or additional data collection.

Overview

Domain Scout AI is a Windows application that helps users discover and evaluate domain-name candidates. This policy explains what information the application and website process.

Domain searches

Search terms and requested domain names may be sent to public DNS services, authoritative domain-registration data services, or registrars to retrieve registration information. These services receive technical request data such as an IP address as part of normal internet communication.

Information we collect

The current website does not require an account and does not intentionally collect names, payment details, or domain-registration contact data. If optional analytics or crash reporting is introduced, this policy will be updated before that functionality is enabled.

Affiliate links

Links to registrars may contain affiliate identifiers. When followed, the registrar or affiliate network may use cookies or similar technologies to attribute a qualifying purchase. Their data practices are controlled by their own privacy policies.

External checkout

Domain registration and payment occur on the selected registrar’s website. Domain Scout AI does not receive or store payment-card information. Information entered at checkout is governed by the registrar’s policies.

Data retention and choices

The current website does not maintain a server-side database of visitors. You may avoid affiliate tracking by not following registrar links from the application. Browser privacy controls may also restrict cookies used after leaving our service.

Security and changes

We use reasonable measures appropriate to the information processed. This policy may change as the product develops; the effective date above will be revised when material changes are made.

Contact

Questions or privacy requests: contact@vortixvpn.com.
